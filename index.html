FAQ

Straight answers. Clean expectations.

Allocations are a premium experience when the rules are clear. Here are the questions we get most.

What does “allocation” mean?

It means inventory is reserved in advance for members. We schedule harvest windows, coordinate your processing, and deliver a consistent experience each release—rather than selling random cuts on a shelf.

Do you ship?

Many farms offer local delivery or freezer drops. If you want nationwide shipping, we can build that—but the premium model is strongest with regional pickup/delivery so experience and product integrity stay high.

Do I get to choose my cuts?

For quarter/half shares, yes—within the processor’s cut sheet options. We provide guidance so you get a balanced box and avoid common first-timer mistakes.

Is it grass-fed/grass-finished?

Your site can state your exact standard here (grass-fed, pasture-raised, finishing protocol, etc.). Luxury brands win by being specific and truthful.
